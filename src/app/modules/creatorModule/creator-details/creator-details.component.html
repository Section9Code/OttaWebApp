Creator overview

To be listed on our creators database there are some things we need from you. Here you can tell us all about you, who you are, your websites, your special media accounts.

Then you can can create your profiles, we recommend you create a different profile for each type of job you want to have. This allows you to work as different types of creative (a writer and an illustrator) without having to confuse the customer. It also allows you to set different rates for different types of work.
